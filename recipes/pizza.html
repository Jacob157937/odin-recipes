The Best Pizza

Few dishes bring people together quite like a homemade pizza, with its golden crust, bubbling cheese, and endless topping possibilities. This recipe guides you through creating a classic pizza from scratch — starting with a simple, flavorful dough and a rich tomato sauce that perfectly complements your favorite ingredients. Whether you're a traditionalist who sticks to pepperoni and mozzarella or someone who loves experimenting with veggies, herbs, and gourmet cheeses, this pizza is the perfect canvas.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or weekend gatherings, this recipe is designed to be both approachable and delicious. With just a bit of time and the right ingredients, you’ll enjoy a pizza that rivals your favorite pizzeria — crisp on the outside, soft and chewy inside, and packed with flavor in every bite.

Home

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basis
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add slices of mozzarella and a few basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of salt.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  7. Cool slightly, then slice and serve.
