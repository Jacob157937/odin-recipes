The Best Waffles

There's something wonderfully nostalgic about the smell of waffles cooking on a lazy morning. Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and full of buttery flavor, these homemade waffles are simple to make and guaranteed to please. Whether you're piling on fresh fruit, syrup, or a dollop of whipped cream, they make breakfast (or brunch) feel a little more special.

This recipe is perfect for quick mornings or relaxed weekends alike. With just a few pantry staples and a waffle iron, you'll be enjoying golden-brown waffles in no time. The batter comes together quickly, and the result is endlessly customizable â€” from sweet toppings to savory add-ins, you can make them your own with ease.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and sugar in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl beat eggs, then mix in milk and melted butter.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring just until smoth.
  5. Lightly grease the waffle iron with butter of non-stick spray.
  6. Pour batter into the wafffle iron and cook until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve hot, with your favourite toppings like syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.
