Best Taco Recipe

The Best Tacos

Tacos are the ultimate customizable meal — quick to make, endlessly versatile, and perfect for satisfying any craving. Whether you’re cooking for a crowd or just a weeknight dinner, these classic tacos strike the perfect balance of flavor and simplicity. With seasoned fillings, fresh toppings, and a warm tortilla to wrap it all up, they’re always a hit.

This recipe keeps things straightforward, giving you a solid base to build on. Whether you go traditional with seasoned ground meat or swap in beans or veggies for a meatless option, the result is a fresh, flavorful taco you can make your own. Just add your favorite extras and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the ground beef.
  2. Cook until browned, breeaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  3. Drain any excess fat, then stir in taco seasoning and a splash of water.
  4. Simmer for a few minutes until the meat is well coated and flavourful.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  6. Assemble the tacos by adding meat, lettuce, tomato, cheese.
  7. Serve immediately, with optional extras like salsa, sour cream, or hot sauce.
